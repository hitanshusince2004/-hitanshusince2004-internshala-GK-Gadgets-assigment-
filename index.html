How big is the projection area?

The projection can cover an entire room depending on distance. For best results, place the projector 1-3 meters from the surface you're projecting onto. The further away, the larger but less intense the projection becomes.

Does it require batteries?

The main unit plugs into a standard power outlet (included), but the remote control requires 2 AAA batteries (not included).

Can I adjust the colors?

Yes! The remote allows you to cycle through 8 different color modes, including a rotating color mode that smoothly transitions between colors.

Is it suitable for adults too?
